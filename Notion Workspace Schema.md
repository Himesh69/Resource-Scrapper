## Notion Workspace Schema 

**Project:** Instagram AI Research & Content Intelligence Platform 

**Version:** 1.0 

**Status:** Production Design 

## Document Overview 

## Purpose 

This document defines the complete Notion workspace structure used to organize research, content planning, AI outputs, workflows, and project management for the Instagram AI Research & Content Intelligence Platform. 

The workspace acts as the operational layer where AI-generated insights, human reviews, content calendars, campaign tracking, and research artifacts are stored and managed. 

The schema is designed to support both automated synchronization through the Notion API and manual collaboration by team members. 

## Objectives 

The Notion workspace is designed to: 

- Centralize all research data 

- Organize AI-generated content 

- Track content production 

- Manage creators and competitors 

- Store reusable prompt templates 

- Build a searchable knowledge base 

- Monitor AI workflows 

- Support team collaboration 

- Maintain project documentation 

- Provide executive dashboards

- Use relations instead of duplicate data. 

- Standardize property names across databases. 

- Archive inactive records rather than deleting them. 

- Keep AI outputs versioned. 

- Review generated content before publishing. 

- Maintain consistent tags and categories.

## Summary 

The Notion workspace schema provides a centralized, structured, and collaborative environment for managing research, creators, trends, content, campaigns, prompts, and AI outputs. Through linked databases, standardized properties, and API-driven automation, the workspace becomes the operational knowledge hub of the Instagram AI Research & Content Intelligence Platform, enabling efficient collaboration between AI agents and human teams while maintaining consistency, traceability, and scalability.
